Tips for Hero Progression

  • Gear your heroes ASAP: Don't wait until late game. As soon as you unlock H1, start farming for starter equipment and runes. Better-geared heroes make campaign progress faster.
  • Build templates matter: See the Heroes section for detailed skill bars and attribute setups for each hero. Copy those builds closely.
  • Level up as you go: Each hero levels with your main character's XP. Just playing through campaign missions will level them, but if you want to farm specific content, ensure heroes have at least base attribute points in their skills.
  • Early game feels slow: With just 1-2 heroes and henchmen, you'll feel underpowered compared to later stages. This is intentional ‚Äì by mid-game you'll have the team to trivialize most content.
  • Don't neglect secondary heroes: H2 (Inept Mesmer) might seem redundant, but the two-hero Mesmer stack's crowd control potential is what enables all your farming. Invest in both equally.
  • Discord Necros are powerful: Once you have all three, the degen output is insane. Weakness + Discord's -2 armor makes every enemy fragile. Most late-game content dies to pure attrition once degen is applied.

Quick reminders so you don‚Äôt have to think mid-mission.

You (Mesmer)

  • Your job is to call targets and shut down casters. Always Ctrl+space a high-priority target (healer / dangerous caster) so Discord Necros and Panic Mesmer focus it with you.
  • Typical opener on a big group: Flag heroes back ‚Üí wand pull ‚Üí once they ball up ‚Üí Energy Surge on your called target ‚Üí Cry of Frustration ‚Üí spam Power Spike / Power Drain / Leech Signet on casters.
  • Position behind your heroes, not in front. You are artillery: let minions + spirits + melee enemies run into your heroes‚Äô line, then drop fire into the ball.
