‚öî How to Play This Team

Quick reminders so you don‚Äôt have to think mid-mission.

You (Mesmer)

  • Your job is to call targets and shut down casters. Always Ctrl+space a high-priority target (healer / dangerous caster) so Discord Necros and Panic Mesmer focus it with you.
  • Typical opener on a big group: Flag heroes back ‚Üí wand pull ‚Üí once they ball up ‚Üí Energy Surge on your called target ‚Üí Cry of Frustration ‚Üí spam Power Spike / Power Drain / Leech Signet on casters.
  • Position behind your heroes, not in front. You are artillery: let minions + spirits + melee enemies run into your heroes‚Äô line, then drop fire into the ball.

Hero Micro & Positioning

  • Flag your heroes at a good chokepoint, then use a longbow / wand to tag a mob and drag it into your kill zone. Once they‚Äôre balled up, unload your fire skills.
  • Avoid face-pulling in HM. Always try to fight one group at a time and watch for patrols. If you accidentally grab two groups, kite back toward your flagged heroes and let spirits/minions soak the hit.
  • Call high-priority targets (healers, dangerous casters). Panic Mesmer + Discord Necros will immediately pile on your called target while you shut them down.

What This Team Handles Well

  • HM Missions & Guardians: Panic + Ineptitude heroes shut down casters and melee, Discord + SoS + your fire nukes clean up. Only tweak for very special missions (escort/split-heavy).
  • Vanquishes: Very solid ‚Äì your job is to group mobs and nuke them. Heroes give you minions, spirits, and shutdown so even big packs are manageable.
  • Dungeons / EotN reps: Spirits + Discord + minion bomber chew through dungeon mobs while you detonate rooms with Searing Flames.

Tweaks

  • Need more defense? Put more HP on you, and swap one Discord Necro to a heavier curses/prot bar, or add Shelter / Union on the Ritualist.
  • Need more damage? Swap Monk to a Ritualist healer and add another offensive Mesmer/Necro hero. Your Ele + triple Necro + double Mesmer becomes pure lawnmower mode.
