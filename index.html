‚öîÔ∏è How to Play This Team

Positioning, pulling, and maximizing your Energy Surge Mesmerway team.

Your Role (Mesmer Player)

  • Call targets with Ctrl+Space ‚Äì your triple E-Surge heroes will focus fire on your called target = instant deletion
  • Typical opener: Flag heroes back ‚Üí wand-pull enemy group ‚Üí when balled up ‚Üí spam Energy Surge + Unnatural Signet on priority targets (monks, ele)
  • Position behind heroes ‚Äì let minions + spirits soak damage, you're artillery in the back

Hero Micro & Pulling

  • Always flag heroes at chokepoints, then use longbow/wand to pull mobs into your kill zone
  • Avoid face-pulling in HM ‚Äì fight one group at a time, watch for patrols
  • BiP Management: Your BiP hero will spam Blood is Power on all 3 E-Surge mesmers = unlimited energy for spam

What This Team Dominates

  • HM Missions: Triple E-Surge deletes caster groups instantly, ST Rit + minions handle melee
  • Vanquishes: Fast, consistent clears with minimal downtime ‚Äì perfect for grinding titles
  • HM Dungeons: ST spirits + minions + triple E-Surge = you can solo most EotN dungeons for rep farming

Adjustments

  • Need more defense? Replace one E-Surge mesmer with Panic Mesmer for more shutdown (use Panic elite instead of E-Surge)
  • Boss fights: Single-target spike is lower ‚Äì use your interrupts on priority skills, let heroes spam E-Surge
