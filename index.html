‚öî How to Play This Team

Quick reminders so you don‚Äôt have to think mid-mission.

You (Dervish)

  • Keep 2‚Äì3 enchants up at all times (Heart of Fury, Vow of Piety, Vital Boon, Ebon Dust Aura). This fuels your armor, blinds, and damage.
  • Open fights with Heart of Fury ‚Üí Aura of Holy Might ‚Üí Mystic Sweep ‚Üí Eremite‚Äôs Attack. That deletes clumps of mobs in HM when heroes are also spiking.
  • Use ‚ÄúSave Yourselves!‚Äù whenever your adrenaline is up and your team is engaged. It‚Äôs basically a short party-wide ‚Äúgod mode‚Äù.

Hero Micro & Positioning

  • Use a bow or long-range spell to pull one group at a time. Don‚Äôt face-pull entire HM mobs when there are patrols nearby.
  • If something is spamming heals / wards / spirits, call it as a target. Two Discord necros + Panic Mesmer will annihilate it fast.

What This Team Handles Well

  • Vanquishes: Slow and safe, especially in areas with lots of melee (Ineptitude + Ebon Dust Aura = perma-blind).
  • Dungeons / EotN reps: SoS + Discord + minions shred dungeons while you carve through the frontline.

Tweaks

  • Need more defense? Turn one Discord Necro into a more defensive curses/prot bar, or bring Shelter/Union on your Rit.
  • Need more damage? Swap Monk into a Ritualist healer and add another offensive Mesmer or Necro.
